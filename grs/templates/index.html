About Us

M. H. Saboo Siddik, also known as MHSS or Saboo Siddik, is an 'A' Grade engineering college located in Byculla, Mumbai, India. Affiliated with the University of Mumbai and accredited by AICTE, it boasts a staff of 154 (comprising 57 teaching and 97 non-teaching members) and serves approximately 1200 students. Established as a Muslim Minority Institution, it owes its creation to the generous donation of Mohammed, the son of Haji Saboo Siddik, who contributed a substantial sum to establish the institution for vocational, technical, and industrial education. Managed and administered by the Anjuman-i-Islam, the college provides various facilities, including workshops, laboratories, classrooms, a seminar hall, library, and reading rooms. Notably, a Seminar Hall was inaugurated in 2001 by dignitaries, Zafar Saifullah IAS and Dr. Raja Ramanna, during the annual day celebrations.

Redressal Process

Register
To initiate the process, faculty members should begin by visiting the login page and selecting the "Sign Up" option. This will prompt them to provide essential registration details, including their name, email address, and contact information. Once these details are submitted, faculty members will have successfully created their account.
Login
With the account created, faculty members can now log in to their personalized dashboard using the credentials they've just established. The dashboard serves as the central control center for all grievance-related activities, providing an intuitive and easy-to-navigate interface.
Lodge grievance
Inside the dashboard, faculty members will find the "Add complaint" section. This is where they can initiate the grievance process. It's important for them to provide a detailed description of the grievance and attach any relevant supporting documents. This step ensures that their concerns are thoroughly understood.
Track grievance
Throughout the grievance process, faculty members can rely on their dashboard for real-time updates on the status of their complaint. They can visit the "Complaints" section to view status of their complaints. Solved complaints are available in the "Solved Complaints" section. From the moment of submission to the final resolution, they will be kept informed at every stage. This transparency is designed to instill confidence in the process.
Download Reports
Once a complaint is successfully resolved, faculty members can access and download the resolution and investigation reports. These documents provide a comprehensive account of the actions taken to address the grievance. They serve as tangible records of the process, ensuring transparency and accountability.
